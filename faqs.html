A: This web-site represents our new tech company, Octive. We will use this site to share development progress & to promote activities within our community.

Q: What is Octive's mission?
A: Octive was founded in February 2025 to build a platform that will revolutionize transactional knowledge sharing Online. In the near future, we will enable any person here on Earth to share any skill they possess with any other person through affordable, on-demand live sessions.

*
"Education for the 21st Century."

Q: How do you all plan to accomplish that?
A: In early April 2025 we will be launching an app on the Google Play Store that will demonstrate the core functionality of our platform. We are so excited to share what we are building with all of you!

*
Q: How can I get involved?
A: The best thing you can do is to start keeping an eye on our other social media profiles as well. Please feel free to ask the staff & community about the project. Above all, start practicing your tutoring skills! We will be thoroughly vetting all new instructors on our platform, so if you would like to participate, please be prepared with a portfolio of print & video materials that demonstrate your skills in whatever subject you plan to offer your expertise in.
